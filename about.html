Meet Molly

Hi! I'm Molly,
Owner and operator of Nychol's Sweets & Things. Thank you for visiting my site to learn more about my small business.

Little About Me

I'm a single momma, work full-time at my "regular" job during the week and if I'm not off watching my daughter dance somewhere, you will find me in the kitchen baking and decorating cakes and things!

How I Got Started

I started making cakes a little over a year ago as a creative outlet and hobby. I have learned much along the way!

It all started by accident one day when I made a cake for a friend's birthday, shared it on Facebook, then other people started asking me to make their cakes. The rest is history, as they say.
